## Domain Overview

Diabetes is a global chronic illness requiring continuous monitoring. Early
detection is critical in preventing severe complications, especially in regions
with limited healthcare infrastructure. Predictive analytics and explainable
AI (XAI) offer decision support to clinicians and frontline health workers.

## Data Context

The project uses the Pima Indians Diabetes dataset, a standard dataset for
classification tasks involving diabetes prediction. It includes measurable
clinical features such as glucose level, BMI, pregnancies, insulin, and age.
